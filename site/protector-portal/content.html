Protector

Protector is FIC’s proprietary online insurance management platform. The platform has been designed for lenders and investors to facilitate quick placement of coverage as either lender-placed or coverage for their assets.

Protector:

  • Can be tailored to the workflow of lenders and property investors.
  • Generates customized compliant notice letters for hazard, flood and collateral.
  • Provides documentation of coverage on your collateral and assets.
  • Reports outline coverage by multiple parameters to help clients categorize their coverage.

  • Coordinates coverage to be ordered and paid on an annual or monthly basis.
